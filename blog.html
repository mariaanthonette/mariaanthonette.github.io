hey, there!

My name's Maria Anthonette Cañaveral.

I was raised in a farm house by my paternal grandparents in Albay, Philippines but grew up particularly inclined with computers and the web.

I'm a mom 24/7 and an accounts receivable by occupation.

I do crochet and crafting to get by during rough days.

By mid of February 2023, I came across an ad about KodeGo's bootcamp on Facebook and had the idea to give my high school love another try - CODING.


Come along in my journey to becoming a full-stack web developer.
